About All-Pro

A small, focused team in Morgantown capturing big moments – on the field, in the studio, and in your home.

Led by photographer Dale Sparks

All-Pro Photography & Framing was founded in 2001 and has been led ever since by photographer Dale Sparks, who has been shooting for more than 47 years.

From a Kodak Instamatic to Big 12 arenas, Dale has spent nearly five decades photographing college athletics and the people who love them.

Over the years, that experience has expanded into portrait work, event coverage, and a full custom framing shop designed to keep everything under one roof.

What makes us “All-Pro”

Sports roots

Our foundation is sports photography – from WVU football and basketball to local teams and special events. We know how to catch the moment that tells the whole story.

Portrait & business work

Seniors, families, business leaders, and staff photos – we create portraits that feel comfortable in the moment and confident on the wall.

Full-service framing

Jerseys, signed pieces, fine art, family photos, and memorabilia – we design custom framing that’s meant to last, not just look good today.

The Dale Sparks Collection

A curated collection of prints and framed pieces celebrating WVU sports and Mountaineer legends – available online and in the shop.

A comfortable, no-drama experience

We keep things simple: clear communication, honest guidance, and a relaxed atmosphere whether you’re in front of the camera or choosing a frame.
