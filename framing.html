Custom Framing

Jerseys, signed prints, tickets, programs, photos, and more – framed so they look good now and last for years.

A full-service frame shop

All-Pro Framing & Photography is a full-service shop for affordable custom framing – from standard sizes to one-of-a-kind pieces and odd shapes.

Whether it’s a signed jersey, a ticket stub and program, or a family portrait, we’ll help you design a layout, choose mats, and select the right frame and glass.

What we frame

Sports & jerseys

Jerseys, signed balls, tickets, and game photos – designed as a single display piece with custom mats and plaques if desired.

Photos & artwork

Family portraits, fine art, posters, and limited-edition prints with a range of mouldings from simple black to wood tones.

Memorabilia & keepsakes

Shadowboxes for medals, programs, records, and three-dimensional items that deserve more than a storage bin.

How it works

  1. Bring your items in. Photos, jerseys, art, or memorabilia – bring it to the shop so we can see it in person.
  2. Design together. We’ll walk you through frame samples, mat colors, and glass options based on where it’ll hang.
  3. We build it. Everything is assembled with care in our shop so it’s ready to hang when you pick it up.

Ready to frame something?

Stop by the shop or reach out with a quick description and we’ll give you a ballpark estimate.
